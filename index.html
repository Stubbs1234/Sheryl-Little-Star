Welcome to sheryl's little stars

OFSTED Rating of "GOOD" Achieved September 2015


My name is Sheryl, and I am Sheryl's Little Stars. I am an Ofsted registered childminder based in Crewe, Cheshire.

I am a member of PACEY (Professional Association for Childcare and Early Years) and have been registered as a childminder since Novbmer 2009. I am fully CRB checked, first aid trained, and I hold an NVQ3 in CCLD (Children's Care, Learning and Development). I have 4 Little Stars of my own, aged 13, 12, and 1.

I have a closed Facebook group (which is only open to parents of cared for children) on which our activites are detailed.

I am flexible with regards to hours, so if you require your child to start a little earlier, or finish later, please contact me.
