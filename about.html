About Me

My name is Sheryl. I am in my thirties and I am a mum of three children (so I have lots of personal experience of children) and I am married to Andy. My children are Jake born in October 2002, Amy born in September 2003 and Chloe born in august 2011. I registered as a childminder in November 2009. I am Ofsted registered and my certificate can be seen hanging on the wall as you step through my front door, along with my liability insurance. Other useful information and posters are here too.

I have also completed the following courses:-

  • NVQ Level 2 - Early Years Care & Education
  • First Aid for Childcarers
  • Basic Food Hygiene
  • ICC
  • Allergen Awareness Course
  • I am also in the process of working towards my NVQ Level 3 Diploma

And I also attend regular training, either in the evenings or at the weekend. Some example of these courses:-

  • Safeguarding
  • Supporting Communication
  • Revised EYFS Briefing
  • EYFS 2 Year Progress Check

I am also:-

  • Registered with the ICO
  • Member of PACEY
  • CRB Checked
